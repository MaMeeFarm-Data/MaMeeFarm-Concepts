# Replication #33
## Compliance Governance
### Operational Alignment

---

## 1. Abstract

Certification confirms authorization.

Compliance ensures correct operation.

Replication #33 establishes compliance governance within DGCP™ to ensure that implementations operate in alignment with structural and licensing requirements.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- define operational compliance standards,
- ensure structural adherence to DGCP architecture,
- preserve governance integrity across implementations,
- prevent structural drift during system operation,
- reinforce license compliance.

---

## 3. Compliance Principles

1. Implementations must follow documented governance structure.
2. Operational behavior must remain consistent with system principles.
3. Structural integrity must remain preserved during operation.
4. Compliance must remain auditable.
5. License conditions must remain enforceable.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Authorization permits adoption.

Compliance sustains legitimacy.

Operational alignment preserves framework credibility.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Certified systems without compliance drift over time.

Compliant systems preserve governance consistency.
