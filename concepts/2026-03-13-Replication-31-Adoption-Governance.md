# Replication #31
## Adoption Governance
### Controlled System Adoption

---

## 1. Abstract

When governance frameworks become visible, external entities may attempt adoption.

Replication #31 establishes governance principles for controlled adoption of the DGCP™ framework.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- regulate framework adoption,
- preserve structural integrity during external implementation,
- ensure license compliance,
- prevent uncontrolled replication,
- maintain governance coherence across implementations.

---

## 3. Adoption Principles

1. Adoption requires explicit authorization.
2. Structural integrity must be preserved.
3. License conditions must remain enforceable.
4. Documentation must remain verifiable.
5. Adoption must remain traceable.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Open visibility does not imply open adoption.

Framework use requires governance alignment.

Controlled adoption preserves system integrity.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Unregulated adoption fragments governance systems.

Controlled adoption preserves structural coherence.
