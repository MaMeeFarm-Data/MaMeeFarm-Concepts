# Replication #45
## System Optimization
### Autonomous Efficiency Layer

---

## 1. Abstract

Systems that improve themselves outperform static systems.

Replication #45 establishes optimization principles within DGCP™ governance to enable autonomous efficiency improvement through continuous feedback and performance validation.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- enhance system efficiency,
- reduce operational friction,
- enable adaptive improvement,
- maintain long-term performance,
- support autonomous system behavior.

---

## 3. Optimization Principles

1. Optimization must be evidence-based.
2. Improvements must be measurable.
3. Changes must remain traceable.
4. System efficiency must be continuously evaluated.
5. Optimization must not violate governance rules.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Systems that optimize based on data become stronger.

Systems that ignore inefficiency become obsolete.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Unoptimized systems decay over time.

Continuously optimized systems sustain performance and relevance.
