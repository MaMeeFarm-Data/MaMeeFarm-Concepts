## 2 Â· Why Scaling Usually Destroys Truth

- replaces local proof with aggregated metrics,  
- optimizes for comparability over honesty.

RWD rejects these trade-offs.

## 4 Â· Replication Architecture

RWD uses five architectural rules:

### **A. Origin Sovereignty**
Each site owns its proof and context.

### **B. Structural Consistency**
Shared schemas enable verification, not conformity.

### **C. Context Isolation**
Local conditions never overwritten by global averages.

### **D. Federated Visibility**
Sites can see each other without central control.

### **E. Non-Hierarchical Expansion**
No site ranks above another by default.

Scaling becomes **federated, not centralized**.

## 5 Â· Replication Principles

### **5.1 Copy the Rules, Not the Results**

### **5.2 Preserve Constraint Signatures**
