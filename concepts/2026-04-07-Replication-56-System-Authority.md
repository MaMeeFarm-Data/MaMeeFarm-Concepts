# Replication #56
## System Authority
### Full Governance Power Layer

---

## 1. Abstract

Authority emerges when structure, data, and influence converge.

Replication #56 establishes system authority principles within DGCP™ governance to define full control, legitimacy, and operational power.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- define system authority,
- establish governance legitimacy,
- enable full control over system operation,
- protect system integrity,
- sustain long-term influence.

---

## 3. Authority Principles

1. Authority must be evidence-based.
2. Control must be structurally defined.
3. Legitimacy must be verifiable.
4. Governance must remain consistent.
5. Authority must not depend on individuals.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Authority without structure is unstable.

Structured authority becomes system power.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems without authority are overridden.

Systems with authority maintain control and direction.
