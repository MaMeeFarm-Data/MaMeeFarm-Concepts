# Replication #21
## Institutionalization Layer
### System Beyond Personality

---

## 1. Abstract

Governance becomes durable when it transcends personality.

Replication #21 formalizes institutionalization within DGCP™ architecture to ensure system continuity beyond individual identity.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- convert governance into institutional structure,
- reduce dependency on founder visibility,
- enable structured succession readiness,
- formalize documented operational standards,
- reinforce structural permanence.

---

## 3. Institutional Principles

1. Rules precede individuals.
2. Documentation precedes authority.
3. Structure precedes narrative.
4. Process precedes personality.
5. License identity remains foundational.

---

## 4. Institutional Logic

Institutionalization does not mean bureaucracy.

It means structure survives absence.

Authority becomes role-based, not personality-based.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems tied to identity are fragile.

Systems tied to documented architecture endure.
