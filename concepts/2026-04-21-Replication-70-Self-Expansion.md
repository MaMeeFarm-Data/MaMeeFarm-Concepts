# Replication #70
## Self-Expansion
### Autonomous System Growth

---

## 1. Abstract

Sustained systems must expand to remain relevant.

Replication #70 establishes self-expansion principles within DGCP™ governance to enable systems to grow autonomously.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- expand system capability,
- extend operational reach,
- maintain growth consistency,
- enable adaptive scaling,
- preserve governance during expansion.

---

## 3. Expansion Principles

1. Growth must preserve core structure.
2. Expansion must be traceable.
3. New components must follow governance rules.
4. Scaling must not degrade system integrity.
5. Expansion must remain controlled and verifiable.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Static systems become obsolete.

Expanding systems remain relevant.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Uncontrolled expansion breaks systems.

Structured self-expansion sustains long-term growth.
