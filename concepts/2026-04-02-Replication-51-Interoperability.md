# Replication #51
## Interoperability
### Controlled System Interaction Layer

---

## 1. Abstract

Systems must interact to operate at scale.

Replication #51 establishes interoperability principles within DGCP™ governance to enable controlled interaction between systems.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- enable cross-system communication,
- ensure compatibility between systems,
- prevent uncontrolled interaction,
- preserve governance boundaries,
- support ecosystem-level operation.

---

## 3. Interoperability Principles

1. System interaction must be controlled.
2. Communication must be documented.
3. Governance boundaries must remain intact.
4. Interaction must not compromise system integrity.
5. Interoperability must be traceable.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Uncontrolled interaction creates system conflict.

Controlled interoperability enables structured integration.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems that interact without governance lose control.

Systems that interact with structure maintain integrity.
