## 2 Â· Why Integration Usually Becomes Extraction

Most integrations fail because they:

- centralize interpretation externally,  
- impose foreign schemas,  
- break provenance chains,  
- convert cooperation into dependency.

IWDS is designed to prevent all five.

## 3 Â· Playbook Definition

IWDS mandates that:

- proofs remain at origin,  
- external systems reference proofs, not copy them,  
- verification occurs through open interfaces,  
- schemas map without rewriting meaning,  
- termination of integration leaves proof intact.

Integration becomes **non-destructive**.
