## 4 Â· Protocol Architecture

The Conflict Containment & Resolution Protocol consists of five stages:

### **A. Early Signal Detection**
Tension, disputes, and misalignment are flagged via proof signals.

### **B. Containment Boundary**
Conflict is isolated to prevent systemic spillover.

### **C. Structured Deliberation Channel**
Parties engage under context-aware, non-dominating rules.

### **D. Resolution Pathways**
Multiple resolution options are explored without forced outcomes.

### **E. Reintegration & Learning**
Resolved conflicts feed back into system improvement.

Conflict becomes **bounded and solvable**.

## 5 Â· Resolution Mechanisms

### **5.1 Proof-Based Issue Framing**

### **5.2 Power-Neutral Facilitation**

### **5.3 Impact-Aware Mediation**

### **5.4 Exit Without Penalty**
Parties may disengage without stigma or exclusion.

### **5.5 Post-Conflict Adjustment**
