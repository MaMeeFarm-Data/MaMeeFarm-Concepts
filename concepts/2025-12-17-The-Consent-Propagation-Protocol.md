# 4 Â· Protocol Architecture

The Consent-Propagation Protocol consists of four layers:

## **A. Consent Declaration Layer**
Humans define permissions, scope, duration, and conditions.

## **B. Consent Binding Layer**
Consent is cryptographically bound to the data and its derivatives.

## **C. Consent Transmission Layer**
Every data transfer carries consent metadata forward.

## **D. Consent Enforcement Layer**
Receiving systems must verify consent before any use.

Together, these layers ensure consent **travels with the data**.
