# Replication #43
## Performance Measurement
### Verifiable Outcome Evaluation

---

## 1. Abstract

Execution must produce measurable results.

Replication #43 establishes performance measurement within DGCP™ to evaluate real-world outcomes through verifiable and traceable metrics.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- define measurable performance indicators,
- evaluate pilot and operational outcomes,
- ensure traceability of results,
- support data-driven decision-making,
- validate system effectiveness.

---

## 3. Measurement Principles

1. Performance must be measurable.
2. Metrics must be verifiable and traceable.
3. Evaluation must rely on recorded data.
4. Outcomes must align with defined use cases.
5. Measurement must remain consistent across implementations.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Execution generates activity.

Measurement generates understanding.

Understanding enables improvement.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Unmeasured systems cannot improve.

Measured systems evolve with clarity.
