# MaMeeFarm™ Project Phases

Date: 2026-03-04  
Document Type: Concept Record

---

## Phase 1 — MaMeeFarm Story & Public Documentation

Period: 2025-04-29 → 2026-03-03

The first phase of the MaMeeFarm™ project focused on documenting daily farm life and real-world activities through public storytelling.

Short-form video was used as the primary medium to record work, environment, and daily routines as part of a **Proof-of-Work and Proof-of-Life archive**.

This phase established the initial public narrative of MaMeeFarm™ and created a historical record of early operations.

All materials produced during this period remain preserved as part of the MaMeeFarm™ historical archive.

---

## Phase 2 — System Development & DGCP™

Start: 2026-03-04

The second phase transitions the project focus from public storytelling toward structured system development.

Operational responsibility is now primarily handled by **P'Toh**, with emphasis on building the underlying architecture of the MaMeeFarm™ data system.

Key areas of work include:

- DGCP™ (Data Governance & Continuous Proof) framework development  
- structured data governance and traceability  
- long-term archival and verification systems  
- real-world evidence recording and data integrity  

This phase represents the evolution of MaMeeFarm™ from a narrative-based documentation project toward a structured system architecture.
