# Replication #69
## Self-Sustaining Loop
### Autonomous Continuous Operation

---

## 1. Abstract

A closed loop enables continuity.

A self-sustaining loop ensures persistence.

Replication #69 establishes principles for continuous autonomous operation within DGCP™ governance.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- sustain continuous system operation,
- maintain closed-loop processes,
- prevent system stagnation,
- enable long-term autonomy,
- reinforce operational continuity.

---

## 3. Sustaining Principles

1. System loops must operate continuously.
2. Feedback must be automatically reintegrated.
3. System must not depend on external triggers.
4. Operation must remain stable over time.
5. Continuity must be preserved across all conditions.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Closed loops maintain alignment.

Self-sustaining loops maintain existence.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems requiring external restart fail over time.

Self-sustaining systems persist.
