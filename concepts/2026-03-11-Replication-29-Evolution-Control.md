# Replication #29
## Evolution Control
### Structured System Change

---

## 1. Abstract

Stable systems must evolve without losing structural integrity.

Replication #29 establishes evolution control principles within DGCP™ governance to allow system improvement while preserving foundational structure.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- regulate structural change,
- prevent uncontrolled modification,
- preserve historical continuity,
- enable documented system evolution,
- maintain governance stability during upgrades.

---

## 3. Evolution Principles

1. Structural evolution must be documented.
2. Changes must preserve foundational rules.
3. Historical records cannot be altered.
4. System upgrades must remain traceable.
5. Governance continuity must be preserved.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Evolution without control creates instability.

Controlled evolution preserves system identity.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Uncontrolled change destroys architecture.

Structured evolution strengthens institutional longevity.
