# Replication #72
## Consistency
### Cross-Instance Structural Alignment

---

## 1. Abstract

Replication without consistency leads to fragmentation.

Replication #72 establishes consistency principles within DGCP™ governance to ensure all system instances remain structurally aligned.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- maintain identical system behavior across instances,
- prevent divergence of governance rules,
- ensure verifiable alignment,
- preserve system identity,
- support reliable distributed operation.

---

## 3. Consistency Principles

1. All instances must follow identical governance rules.
2. System behavior must be reproducible.
3. Divergence must be detectable.
4. Alignment must be verifiable.
5. Updates must propagate consistently.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Distributed systems without consistency fragment.

Aligned systems scale reliably.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Inconsistent systems lose integrity.

Consistent systems maintain identity and trust.
