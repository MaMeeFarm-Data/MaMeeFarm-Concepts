## 1 Â· Abstract

**Traceability Without Surveillance (TWS)** defines how proof-based systems provide **verifiable provenance and auditability** without requiring continuous monitoring, behavioral profiling, or centralized observation.

TWS separates **traceability of evidence** from **surveillance of people**.

---

## 2 Â· Why Surveillance Creeps In

Traceability systems become surveillance when they:

- log continuously by default,  
- bind identity to every action,  
- centralize observation,  
- infer behavior beyond evidence,  
- optimize for control instead of proof.

TWS rejects inference beyond recorded proof.

---

## 3 Â· Implementation Definition

TWS mandates that:

- traceability applies to proof, not people,  
- only declared events are recorded,  
- identities are optional and minimal,  
- aggregation does not create profiles,  
- no hidden observation occurs.

What is not recorded  
**cannot be inferred**.

---

## 4 Â· Traceability Architecture

TWS operates through five components:

### **A. Proof-Bound Traceability**
Only recorded proofs are traceable.

### **B. Event-Scoped Logging**
No background or ambient tracking.

### **C. Minimal Identity Binding**
Actors are referenced only as needed.

### **D. Decentralized Inspection**
No central watcher exists.

### **E. Anti-Inference Boundaries**
Systems do not extrapolate behavior.

---

## 5 Â· Design Principles

### **5.1 Proof Over Presence**
Evidence matters, not continuous visibility.

### **5.2 Explicit Beats Implicit**
Only intentional records exist.

### **5.3 No Behavioral Models**
Systems do not guess.

### **5.4 Audit Without Watching**
Verification does not require monitoring.

### **5.5 Power-Resistant Design**
No observer gains leverage.

---

## 6 Â· Human-First Safeguards

TWS ensures that:

- daily life is not monitored,  
- participation does not create dossiers,  
- people are not profiled,  
- silence remains private,  
- dignity survives verification.

For **MaMeeFarmâ„¢**:

> We prove what we did,

## 7 Â· Validation Criteria

Traceability Without Surveillance is valid when:

- proofs are auditable end-to-end,  
- no continuous tracking exists,  
- identities are not aggregated,  
- observers cannot reconstruct lives,  
- verification does not feel invasive.

Trust grows when privacy remains intact.
