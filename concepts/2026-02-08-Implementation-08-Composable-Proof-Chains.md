CPC mandates that:

## 4 Â· Proof Chain Architecture

CPC operates through five components:

### **A. Atomic Proof Units**
Each proof stands alone.

### **B. Explicit Link References**
Chains are declared, not inferred.

### **C. Directional Integrity**
Links preserve temporal order.

### **D. Multi-Chain Compatibility**
One proof may belong to many chains.

### **E. No Global Narrative Lock**
Chains do not enforce meaning.

## 5 Â· Design Principles

### **5.1 Independence First**

### **5.2 Linking Is Additive**

### **5.3 No Forced Canon**

### **5.4 Reversible Composition**
Chains can be reassembled or dissolved.

### **5.5 Failure Isolation**
Broken links do not corrupt proof.
