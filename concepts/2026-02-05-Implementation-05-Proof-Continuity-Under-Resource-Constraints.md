## 2 Â· Why Scarcity Breaks Most Systems

Systems fail under constraint because they:

- require constant attention,  
- depend on new hardware,  
- punish missed entries,  
- collapse when effort drops.

PCURC assumes **effort fluctuates**.

## 3 Â· Implementation Definition

PCURC mandates that:

- proof can be reduced without breaking continuity,  
- low-frequency capture remains valid,  
- absence is recorded honestly,  
- degraded modes are first-class,  
- recovery does not rewrite gaps.

Continuity means **honest presence**, not density.

## 4 Â· Constraint-Resilient Architecture

PCURC operates through five mechanisms:

### **A. Degraded Capture Modes**
Text-only, single photo, or marker-based proof is valid.

### **B. Energy-Aware Operation**
Systems adapt to power availability.

### **C. Time-Light Workflows**
Capture fits into spare moments.

### **D. Explicit Scarcity Annotation**
Resource limits are recorded as context.

### **E. Graceful Recovery**
Normal operation resumes without backfilling fiction.
