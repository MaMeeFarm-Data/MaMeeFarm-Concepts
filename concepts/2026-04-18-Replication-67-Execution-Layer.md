# Replication #67
## Execution Layer
### Autonomous System Action

---

## 1. Abstract

Decision without action has no outcome.

Replication #67 establishes execution principles within DGCP™ governance to enable systems to perform actions based on verified decisions.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- execute system decisions,
- perform autonomous actions,
- maintain operational continuity,
- ensure traceable execution,
- connect decision to real-world outcomes.

---

## 3. Execution Principles

1. Actions must follow verified decisions.
2. Execution must be traceable.
3. Actions must preserve governance integrity.
4. Execution must be consistent with system rules.
5. Outcomes must be recorded and verifiable.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Systems that decide but do not act remain theoretical.

Systems that act become real.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Non-executing systems stagnate.

Executing systems evolve through real-world interaction.
