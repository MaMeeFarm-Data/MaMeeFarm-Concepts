# Replication #61
## Self-Sovereign System
### Autonomous Governance Layer

---

## 1. Abstract

The highest level of a system is independence.

Replication #61 establishes self-sovereign principles within DGCP™ governance to define systems that operate without dependency on external authority.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- establish system independence,
- remove reliance on external control,
- ensure self-governed operation,
- preserve integrity across all conditions,
- define ultimate system autonomy.

---

## 3. Sovereign Principles

1. Governance must operate independently.
2. Authority must be internally verifiable.
3. System operation must not depend on external entities.
4. Integrity must be preserved without external enforcement.
5. System continuity must be self-sustained.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Dependent systems can be controlled.

Independent systems define their own operation.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems dependent on external authority can be overridden.

Self-sovereign systems remain intact regardless of external conditions.
