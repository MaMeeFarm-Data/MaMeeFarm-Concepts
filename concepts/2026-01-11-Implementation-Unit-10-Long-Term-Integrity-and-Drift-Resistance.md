## 2 Â· Why Systems Drift

Even well-designed systems drift because:

- shortcuts accumulate under pressure,  
- new participants inherit habits, not principles,  
- incentives slowly override values,  
- exceptions become norms,  
- documentation lags behind reality.

Drift is gradual, invisible, and rarely malicious.

- shortcuts leave traces,  
- correction is automatic, not discretionary.

Integrity becomes **self-maintaining**.
