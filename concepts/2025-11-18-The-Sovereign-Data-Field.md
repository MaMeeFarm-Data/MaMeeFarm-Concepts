## 2 Â· Defining Sovereign Truth  

A data object becomes *sovereign* when it possesses:

1. **Self-contained proof**  
   (hash, timestamp, origin trace, irreversible evidence)

2. **Boundary of use**  
   (a license defining its rights and protections)

3. **Independence from platforms**  
   (valid even if Opensea, GitHub, or blockchains change)

4. **Human-origin authority**  
   (meaning is grounded in real work, not algorithmic fabrication)

Sovereignty means the truth no longer needs endorsement to remain true.
