# Replication #46
## Resilience & Adaptation
### System Survival Under Change

---

## 1. Abstract

Systems that cannot adapt eventually fail.

Replication #46 establishes resilience and adaptation principles within DGCP™ governance to ensure system survival under changing conditions.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- maintain system continuity under stress,
- enable adaptive response to change,
- preserve operational stability,
- prevent system collapse,
- ensure long-term survivability.

---

## 3. Resilience Principles

1. Systems must withstand disruption.
2. Adaptation must be evidence-based.
3. Structural integrity must be preserved.
4. Recovery must be traceable.
5. Adaptation must not violate governance rules.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Rigid systems break.

Adaptive systems survive.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems that cannot adapt collapse.

Systems that adapt within structure endure.
