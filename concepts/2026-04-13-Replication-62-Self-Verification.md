# Replication #62
## Self-Verification
### Autonomous Integrity Validation

---

## 1. Abstract

Independence without verification leads to failure.

Replication #62 establishes self-verification principles within DGCP™ governance to ensure continuous internal validation of system integrity.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- validate system integrity internally,
- detect structural inconsistency,
- prevent silent failure,
- maintain trust without external audit,
- sustain autonomous governance.

---

## 3. Verification Principles

1. Validation must be continuous.
2. Verification must be evidence-based.
3. System state must be observable.
4. Errors must be detectable.
5. Integrity must be provable at all times.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Unverified systems drift.

Self-verifying systems remain aligned.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems that cannot verify themselves degrade silently.

Systems that self-verify maintain long-term integrity.
