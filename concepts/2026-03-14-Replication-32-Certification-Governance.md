# Replication #32
## Certification Governance
### Verified System Adoption

---

## 1. Abstract

Framework adoption requires verification.

Replication #32 establishes certification governance within DGCP™ to ensure that only verified implementations may claim official alignment with the framework.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- verify authorized framework adoption,
- prevent unauthorized claims of system implementation,
- maintain governance credibility,
- ensure structural compliance across deployments,
- preserve institutional trust.

---

## 3. Certification Principles

1. Certification requires formal authorization.
2. Implementations must preserve structural integrity.
3. Certification must be traceable and documented.
4. Unauthorized claims of certification are prohibited.
5. Certification status must remain verifiable.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Framework visibility invites imitation.

Certification distinguishes verified adoption from imitation.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Unverified adoption weakens system credibility.

Verified certification preserves institutional trust.
