## 3 Â· Interface Definition

IPT mandates that:

## 4 Â· Transfer Architecture

IPT operates through five components:

### **A. Proof Immutability**

### **B. Context Preservation**

### **C. Interpretive Documentation**
How to read the proof is recorded.

### **D. Temporal Verification**
Future actors can verify without trust in the past.

### **E. Authority Decay**
No role retains power across generations.
