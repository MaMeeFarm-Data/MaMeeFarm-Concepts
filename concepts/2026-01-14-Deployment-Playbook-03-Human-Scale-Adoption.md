## 2 Â· Why Adoption Breaks People

Most deployments fail because they:

- equate adoption with compliance,  
- ignore fatigue and cognitive load,  
- reward volume over consistency,  
- treat humans as throughput,  
- confuse short-term uptake with success.

HSA treats humans as **the limiting factor to respect**, not a bottleneck to remove.

## 3 Â· Playbook Definition

Human-Scale Adoption mandates that:

- participation remains voluntary at all stages,  
- workload increases only after stability,  
- drop-offs are acceptable and visible,  
- rest and pauses are valid states,  
- no penalties exist for slower rhythms.

Adoption becomes **inhabitable**, not extractive.

- weekly fluctuation in capacity,  
- seasonal changes in workload,  
- life events and interruptions,  
- aging and health constraints.

## 5 Â· Growth Principles

### **5.1 Consistency Over Volume**

### **5.2 Opt-In Expansion**
