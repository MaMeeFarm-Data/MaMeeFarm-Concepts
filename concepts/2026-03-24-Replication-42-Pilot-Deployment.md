# Replication #42
## Pilot Deployment
### Controlled Real-World Testing

---

## 1. Abstract

Execution begins with controlled testing.

Replication #42 establishes pilot deployment principles within DGCP™ to enable real-world validation under controlled conditions.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- initiate real-world testing,
- validate use cases in controlled environments,
- minimize operational risk,
- collect verifiable performance data,
- prepare for scalable deployment.

---

## 3. Pilot Principles

1. Pilot scope must remain clearly defined.
2. Deployment must remain controlled and limited.
3. All activities must generate verifiable proof.
4. Structural integrity must be preserved.
5. Pilot results must be documented and auditable.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Concept proves possibility.

Pilot proves reality.

Controlled testing enables safe execution.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Uncontrolled deployment risks system failure.

Controlled pilot preserves system integrity while enabling validation.
