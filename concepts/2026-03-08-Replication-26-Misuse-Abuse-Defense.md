# Replication #26
## Misuse & Abuse Defense
### Structural Protection Layer

---

## 1. Abstract

When systems gain visibility, misuse becomes inevitable.

Replication #26 establishes defense principles to protect the DGCP™ framework from structural misuse and abuse.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- prevent unauthorized framework extraction,
- detect misuse of governance structure,
- defend against misrepresentation of system origin,
- protect intellectual integrity,
- preserve structural coherence.

---

## 3. Defense Principles

1. Structural extraction without authorization is prohibited.
2. Misrepresentation of system origin is prohibited.
3. Partial replication cannot claim system equivalence.
4. Governance implementation requires formal authorization.
5. Documentation remains the primary evidence of authenticity.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Visibility invites imitation.

Imitation invites distortion.

Structural defense preserves authenticity.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Unprotected systems are gradually fragmented through imitation.

Protected systems preserve structural identity.
