# Replication #58
## Standardization
### Global Governance Standard Layer

---

## 1. Abstract

Systems achieve maximum impact when they become standards.

Replication #58 establishes standardization principles within DGCP™ governance to define how systems transition into globally recognized frameworks.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- define system standardization,
- enable global adoption,
- ensure consistency across implementations,
- maintain governance integrity at scale,
- establish long-term system recognition.

---

## 3. Standardization Principles

1. System rules must be consistent.
2. Implementation must be reproducible.
3. Governance must remain verifiable.
4. Adoption must preserve core structure.
5. Standards must evolve without losing identity.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Unstandardized systems remain isolated.

Standardized systems become infrastructure.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems without standards fragment.

Systems that become standards sustain global relevance.
