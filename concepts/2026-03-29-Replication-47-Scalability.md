# Replication #47
## Scalability
### System Expansion Without Structural Loss

---

## 1. Abstract

Systems that cannot scale remain limited.

Replication #47 establishes scalability principles within DGCP™ governance to enable system expansion without compromising structural integrity.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- enable system growth,
- maintain governance integrity during expansion,
- prevent structural degradation,
- support multi-entity deployment,
- preserve system identity at scale.

---

## 3. Scalability Principles

1. System expansion must preserve core rules.
2. Governance structure must remain consistent.
3. Scaling must be traceable and controlled.
4. Expansion must not introduce ambiguity.
5. Structural integrity must remain intact.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Growth without structure leads to fragmentation.

Structured scalability preserves system identity.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Uncontrolled expansion breaks systems.

Controlled scalability strengthens system reach.
