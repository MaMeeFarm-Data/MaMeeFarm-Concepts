# Replication #66
## Decision Layer
### Autonomous System Decision-Making

---

## 1. Abstract

Prediction without decision has no effect.

Replication #66 establishes decision-making principles within DGCP™ governance to enable systems to act based on verified data and anticipation.

---

## 2. Purpose

This layer exists to:

- enable autonomous decision-making,
- act on verified data,
- utilize learned patterns,
- respond proactively to system conditions,
- maintain operational continuity.

---

## 3. Decision Principles

1. Decisions must be evidence-based.
2. Decisions must incorporate learning.
3. Decisions must consider anticipated outcomes.
4. Decision logic must be traceable.
5. Decisions must preserve governance integrity.

---

## 4. Governance Logic

Systems that cannot decide remain passive.

Systems that decide become active agents.

---

## 5. Stability Rule

Systems without decision capability stall.

Systems with structured decision-making sustain operation and progress.
